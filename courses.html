Intermediate

The intermediate level typically refers to a stage of learning or proficiency between beginner and advanced. It indicates that the learner has a solid foundation of skills or knowledge but is still developing more advanced abilities. This stage often involves refining techniques and deepening understanding.

B-Tech

B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) is a 4-year undergraduate degree focused on engineering and technology. It combines theoretical knowledge and practical skills in fields like computer science, mechanical, electrical, and civil engineering. Graduates can pursue careers in industries, research, or higher studies in specialized engineering domains.

Post Graduate

A postgraduate degree is an advanced academic qualification pursued after completing a bachelor's degree. It can be a master's, diploma, or doctorate, focusing on specialized knowledge and skills in a particular field. Postgraduate programs typically enhance career prospects, research opportunities, and subject expertise.
